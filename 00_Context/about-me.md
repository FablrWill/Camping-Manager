# About Me

## Who I Am

I'm Will Sink, 42 years old, currently living in Asheville, NC. I moved here from Durham in January 2026 after my marriage ended. The last six months have been mostly about getting through the divorce and finding my footing again.

## What I Do

I'm co-founding Fablr.ai with my brother Jim. Fablr is a voice-first AI biographer platform — users have a conversation with an AI ghostwriter named Thomas, and it transforms their spoken memories into polished, authentic narratives. The idea came from watching our dad use a competitor's product to record his life story. We saw the value but also the limitations — the process was tedious and writing-heavy. Fablr removes that barrier by letting people just talk.

I'm the vision and strategy side. Jim is the full-time developer. I left my job to work on Fablr full-time, but after my wife left, I stepped away for about six months. I haven't been deeply involved since roughly mid-2025, and I'm concerned there's a gap between what Jim has built and what I originally had in mind.
